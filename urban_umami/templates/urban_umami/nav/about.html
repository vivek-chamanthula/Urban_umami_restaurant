Our Story

Welcome to Urban Umami, where flavours meet feelings and every dish tells a story.

What started as a simple dream—to bring diverse global flavours under one warm, welcoming roof—soon turned into a delicious journey of passion, creativity, and unforgettable taste.

At Urban Umami, we believe food is more than just something you eat. It’s a memory. A moment. A feeling. From the bold spices of India, to the soulful comfort of Chinese cuisine, to the rich elegance of Continental dishes… every flavour on our menu is crafted to spark joy with every bite.

Our chefs come together like a family, combining tradition, innovation, and a love for extraordinary food. Each dish is prepared with care, freshness, and that special touch of umami—the taste that lingers long after the meal is done. Whether you’re here for a cosy dinner, a quick bite, or a celebration with your favourite people, Urban Umami is designed to make you feel at home.

Here, every flavour has a story. And now, you are a part of it.

WHY WE EXIST - URBAN UMAMI

Urban Umami exists to celebrate the universal joy of flavour. We bring Indian spice, Chinese warmth, and Continental balance to one table. Our purpose is simple — create food that makes people feel alive.

We believe every dish should carry emotion, comfort, and honesty. That’s why we craft each plate with fresh ingredients and soulful cooking. We exist to turn everyday meals into meaningful moments.

Our mission is to serve quality that never compromises and taste that never fades. To welcome every guest like family and make every bite worth the visit. Urban Umami exists to make flavour a beautiful experience, every single time..
