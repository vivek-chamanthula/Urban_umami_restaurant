Privacy Policy — Urban Umami

At Urban Umami, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your personal information when you visit our website or use our services.

1.Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Personal details — Name, phone number, email, and address.
  • Order information — Food preferences, order history, and delivery details.
  • Payment details — UPI, card details (processed securely through payment gateways, not stored by us).
  • Technical data — IP address, browser type, and device information for security and analytics.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used only to:

  • Process reservations, orders, and deliveries.
  • Improve customer experience and service quality.
  • Contact you regarding orders, feedback, or support.
  • Send updates about offers (only if you opt-in).
  • Maintain website security and prevent fraudulent use.

3. Data Protection & Security

  • Your information is stored securely and protected from unauthorized access.
  • Payment information is handled by trusted third-party payment gateways using encryption
  • We never sell or share your personal information with external companies for marketing.

4.Sharing of Information

We may share limited information with:

  • Delivery partners — only name, address, and phone number for order delivery.
  • Payment processors — only payment-related information, handled securely.
  • Authorities — only when legally required for safety or verification.

We never share your data for advertising or commercial sale.

5. Cookies & Website Tracking

  • Request access to the information we hold about you.
  • Our website may use cookies to improve user experience and load pages faster..
  • Cookies help us understand which menu items or pages customers view most.
  • You can disable cookies anytime in your browser settings.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Users must not misuse the website by spreading malware, hacking attempts, or fake orders.
  • All text, images, logos, and design on this website belong to Urban Umami and cannot be copied without permission.
  • User data submitted through forms will be kept confidential and used only for service-related purposes.

7. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external sites. Urban Umami is not responsible for the privacy practices of those websites.

8.Updates to This Policy
